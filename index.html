Welcome to Our Concrete World. This website showcases my course portfolio and project. The project demonstrates the benefits of urban density in the form of efficiency and innovation. Cities are some of the oldest and greatest human inventions. People have lived in Athens since 5000B.C and nowadays Tokoyo is home to 37 million people. It is a mistake to think that cities will not survive the Coronavirus pandemic. Cities such as London and New York have survivied pandemics, wars, terrorism and natural disasters. The Concrete jungle project uses data to show its benefits.
